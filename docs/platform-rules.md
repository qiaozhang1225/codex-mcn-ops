# Platform Rules

These are initial guardrails for publish package preparation. They should be refined against real device behavior.

## Douyin

- Package: `com.ss.android.ugc.aweme`
- Title max: 30 characters
- Body max: 1000 characters
- Hashtags max: 5

## Xiaohongshu

- Package: `com.xingin.xhs`
- Title max: 20 characters
- Body max: 1000 characters
- Hashtags max: 20

## WeChat Channels

- Package: `com.tencent.mm`
- Body max: 1000 characters
- Hashtags max: 10
- Publishing is inside WeChat, so navigation must be calibrated on the target phone.

## Kuaishou

- Package: `com.smile.gifmaker`
- Body max: 500 characters
- Hashtags max: 3
- Cover constraints should be checked during dry-run.
